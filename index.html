Estimation comments (1)

$$\log E = \log C(S,W,P,N) - \log e(M,H,D)$$
  • $C$ is short-term (variable) costs
    (ignores capital and managerial inputs)
  • $E$ excludes transportation and food
  • $e$ is efficiency (managerial?)
  • $S$ is test scores, graduation rates, or college-bound
